Experiences of K.Dot

Kendrick Lamar is considered by many as one of the most influential musical artists, especially in rap, hip hop, and in the 21st century. He has created an outstanding legacy of political and racial activism throughout many cultures, predominately with the African American culture. Gaining such an amazing and coveted award as the Pulitzer Prize for Music really cemented him as one of th greats in terms of music and lyricism. Growing up in Comptom, California, USA exposed Lamar to many dangerous people and situations, many of which he would not shy away from. However, through the hardships and struggles he would go through living in poverty surrounding by gang violence, Lamar continued to hold his dream of rapping and creating meaningful and pwoerful music to inspire.
