About Kendrick Lamar Duckworth

Born June 17, 1987 as Kendrick Lamar Duckworth, Kendrick Lamar grew up in Compton, California, USA. He was the first child of Kenneth Duckworth and Paula Oliver who were both from Chicago. Before Kendrick was born, they would move to Compton due to Kenneth's affiliation with the Gangster Disciples, a prominent street gang in the 1960s.

Kendrick's name comes from singer-songwriter Eddie Kendricks, one of the former lead singers for the group The Temptations.

Kendrick would be an onyl child until when he turned seven years old. His parents would then have his two younger brothers and younger sister, Kayla Sawyer. Other famous relatives of Kendrick's are his cousin Nick Young, an NBA player for the Washington Wizards, and Hykeem Jamaal Carter Jr., also known as Baby Keem, another popular rapper.
