Skills and Awards of Kung Fu Kenny

K.Dot has been performing as a rapper since 2003; while in high school, he would compete in rap battles and use beats and music from that time and record his freestyles over them. Each album he has released and his entire discography has been met with overwhelming praise. Some of his songs and albums have even been used in university level courses for research on critical race topics.

Kendrick Lamar has garnered numerous awards and accolades from his long career as a rapper and musician. A list of most of his awards is provided below:

  1. 17 Grammy Awards, third-most by a rapper
  2. 1 Primetime Emmy Award
  3. 29 BET Hip Hop Awards, the msot by any artist
  4. 11 MTV Video Music Awards, that includes 2 Video of the Year wins
  5. 6 Billboard Music Awards
  6. and, a Pulitzer Prize for Music, for his fourth studio album Damn
